Layerly – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025
Last Updated: March 22, 2026

1. Overview

Layerly is committed to protecting your privacy. The vast majority of your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Clothing preferences and wardrobe items you add to the App
  • Comfort baseline and occasion preferences
  • Trip destinations and activity types you enter

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device location (only when you grant permission, used solely to fetch local weather data)
  • City names you search for weather or travel planning

Camera & Photos

  • The camera may be used to take photos of your clothing items for your wardrobe
  • Photos selected from your library may be attached to wardrobe items
  • All photos are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or account credentials
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not build advertising profiles
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Location & City Data — sent to WeatherAPI.com to retrieve weather conditions for your location or searched city. No personally identifying information is included.
  • Wardrobe & Preference Data — stored exclusively on your device to generate outfit recommendations. This data is never uploaded to any server.
  • Trip Data — stored locally to generate daily outfit recommendations and packing lists.
  • Camera & Photos — used solely to let you attach clothing photos to wardrobe items. Photos remain on your device at all times.

4. Third-Party Services

WeatherAPI.com — Weather data is fetched from WeatherAPI.com. Your city name or geographic coordinates are sent to their servers. Please review their Privacy Policy for details.

We do not share your wardrobe, preferences, photos, or any other personal information with any third party.

5. Data Storage & Security

All wardrobe items, clothing photos, preferences, and trip plans are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard iOS data storage, protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). Deleting the App permanently removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Layerly is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices

  • Location Access — Revoke at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Layerly. The App still works with a manually entered city.
  • Camera Access — Revoke at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → Layerly. You can still add photos from your photo library.
  • Photo Library Access — Revoke at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Layerly.
  • Delete Your Data — Deleting the App removes all locally stored data, including any clothing photos.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
